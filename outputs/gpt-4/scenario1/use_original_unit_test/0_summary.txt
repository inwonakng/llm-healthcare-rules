```
1. The policy covers injectable osteoporosis drugs.
2. The coverage is applicable if the following conditions are met:
   2.1. The beneficiary is a woman with osteoporosis who qualifies for the Medicare home health benefit.
   2.2. The beneficiary has a bone fracture that a doctor certifies was related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
   2.3. The beneficiary's doctor certifies that she can't administer the injection herself or learn how to do so.
3. Medicare will also cover the services of a home health nurse or aide to provide the injection if:
   3.1. The beneficiary's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the drug by injection, as per requirement 2.3.
```
