Summary:

Medicare provides coverage for injectable osteoporosis drugs under certain conditions which comprise:

1. The recipient must be a woman with osteoporosis who meets the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit.
2. The woman must have a bone fracture certified by a doctor as related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
3. A doctor must certify that the patient is incapable of self-injecting the drug or learning to administer it themselves.
4. Medicare will also cover a home health nurse or aide to administer the injection if the patient's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to provide the injection.
