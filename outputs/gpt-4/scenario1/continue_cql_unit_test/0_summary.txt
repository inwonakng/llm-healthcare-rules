1. The policy covers injectable osteoporosis drugs.
2. The coverage is applicable if the policyholder is a woman with osteoporosis who qualifies for the Medicare home health benefit.
3. The policyholder must have a bone fracture that a doctor certifies was related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
4. The doctor must certify that the policyholder is unable to self-administer the injection or learn how to self-administer the drug.
5. Medicare will also cover the services of a home health nurse or aide to administer the injection if the policyholder's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to do so.
