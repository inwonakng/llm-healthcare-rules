1. Medicare covers the provision of Intravenous Immune Globulin (IVIG) at home for patients diagnosed with primary immune deficiency disease.
2. The decision for home-based IVIG treatment must be deemed medically appropriate by a doctor. 
3. Medicare Part B covers the cost of IVIG itself. 
4. Medicare does not cover additional costs of other items and services related to the home-based IVIG treatment.
