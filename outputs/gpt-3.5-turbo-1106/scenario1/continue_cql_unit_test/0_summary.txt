1. Medicare covers injectable osteoporosis drugs if the following conditions are met:
    1.1. The patient is a woman with osteoporosis who qualifies for the Medicare home health benefit.
    1.2. The patient has experienced a bone fracture certified by a doctor to be related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
    1.3. The patient's doctor certifies that the patient is unable to self-administer the injection or learn to do so, and requires assistance.
2. If the patient's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the injection, Medicare will cover the cost of a home health nurse or aide to provide the injection.
