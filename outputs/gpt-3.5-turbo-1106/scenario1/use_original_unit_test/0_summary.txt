1. Medicare covers injectable osteoporosis drugs if the following conditions are met:
    1.1. The individual is a woman with osteoporosis who qualifies for the Medicare home health benefit.
    1.2. The individual has experienced a bone fracture certified by a doctor to be related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
    1.3. The individual's doctor certifies that they are unable to self-administer the injection or learn how to do so.
2. If the individual's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the injection, Medicare will cover the home health nurse or aide to provide the injection.
