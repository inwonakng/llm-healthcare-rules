1. Medicare covers injectable osteoporosis drugs for women with osteoporosis who meet the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit.
2. Coverage is provided if the individual has a bone fracture certified by a doctor to be related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
3. The individual's doctor must certify that they are unable to self-administer the injection or learn how to do so.
4. If the individual's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the injection, Medicare will cover the home health nurse or aide to provide it.
