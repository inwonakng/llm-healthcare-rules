Summary of requirements for Medicare coverage of injectable osteoporosis drugs:

1. You must be a woman with osteoporosis who meets the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit.
2. You must have a bone fracture that a doctor certifies was related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
3. Your doctor must certify that you are unable to give yourself the injection or learn how to do it.
4. If your family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the injection, Medicare will cover a home health nurse or aide to provide it.

Note: Medicare covers injectable osteoporosis drugs if all of the above conditions are met.
