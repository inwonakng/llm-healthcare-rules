Summary of Insurance Policy Document:

1. Medicare covers Intravenous Immune Globulin (IVIG) if you have a diagnosis of primary immune deficiency disease.
2. IVIG can be provided in your home if your doctor determines it is medically appropriate.
3. Medicare Part B covers the cost of the IVIG itself.
4. Medicare does not cover other items and services related to receiving IVIG at home.
