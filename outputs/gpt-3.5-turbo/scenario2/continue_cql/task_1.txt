High-level summary: Medicare covers Intravenous Immune Globulin (IVIG) treatment in your home if you have primary immune deficiency disease and your doctor deems it necessary. 

Requirements:
1. You must have a diagnosis of primary immune deficiency disease.
2. Your doctor must determine that receiving IVIG at home is medically appropriate for you.
3. Medicare Part B covers the cost of IVIG itself.
4. Medicare does not cover other items and services associated with receiving IVIG at home.
