Summary: Injectable osteoporosis drugs may be covered by Medicare for women who meet certain conditions related to their health and circumstances. 

Requirements: 
1. The woman must have osteoporosis and meet the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit.
2. The woman must have a bone fracture certified by a doctor as being related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
3. The woman's doctor must certify that she is unable to administer the injection herself or learn how to do so.
4. If the woman's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the drug by injection, Medicare will cover the cost of a home health nurse or aide to provide the injection.
