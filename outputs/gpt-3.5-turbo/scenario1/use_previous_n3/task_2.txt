@prefix rdf: <http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns#> .
@prefix rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#> .

### Rule 1: The patient must be a woman with osteoporosis
{?patient rdf:type :Patient .
?patient :hasGender :female .
?patient :hasCondition :osteoporosis .}

### Rule 2: The patient must meet the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit
{?patient rdf:type :Patient .
?patient :meetsCriterion :medicareHomeHealthBenefit .}

### Rule 3: The patient must have a bone fracture certified by a doctor as related to post-menopausal osteoporosis
{?patient rdf:type :Patient .
?patient :hasCondition :postMenopausalOsteoporosis .
?patient :hasCertifiedFracture ?fracture .
?fracture rdf:type :BoneFracture .}

### Rule 4: The patient's doctor must certify that they are unable to self-administer the injection or learn how to do so
{?patient rdf:type :Patient .
?patient :hasDoctor ?doctor .
?doctor :certifiesInability ?patient .
?doctor :certifiesInabilityToLearn ?patient .}

### Rule 5: If family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the drug, Medicare will cover the home health nurse or aide to provide the injection
{?patient rdf:type :Patient .
?patient :hasFamilyOrCaregiver ?familyOrCaregiver .
?familyOrCaregiver :unableOrUnwillingToAdminister ?drug .
?patient :hasHealthCover :medicare .
?medicare :willCover ?injection .
?injection :providedBy :homeHealthNurseOrAide .}
