1. Injectable osteoporosis drugs are covered by Medicare under certain conditions. 
2. The coverage applies only to women with osteoporosis who meet the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit.
3. A physician must certify that the patient has experienced a bone fracture related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
4. The patient's doctor must certify that the patient is incapable of self-administering the injections or learning how to do so. 
5. If family or caregivers can't or won't administer the injection, Medicare will also cover the cost for a home health nurse or aide to provide the injection, referring back to requirement 4.
