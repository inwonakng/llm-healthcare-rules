1. Medicare covers Intravenous Immune Globulin (IVIG) if the patient is diagnosed with primary immune deficiency disease.
2. The decision on the medical appropriateness of IVIG for the patient must be made by their doctor.
3. IVIG must be administered in the patient's home for coverage. 
4. Medicare Part B covers the cost of IVIG only.
5. Other items and services associated with home administration of IVIG are not covered under Medicare.
