Requirements for Medicare Coverage of Injectable Osteoporosis Drugs:

1. Be a woman with osteoporosis who meets the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit.
2. Have a bone fracture that a doctor certifies was related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
3. Not be able to give yourself the injection or learn how to give yourself the drug by injection.
4. Have a home health nurse or aide provide the injection if your family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to give you the drug by injection.
5. Meet all of the above requirements to be eligible for coverage under Medicare.
