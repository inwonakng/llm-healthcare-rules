`. All requirements for injectable osteoporosis drugs covered by Medicare are as follows:

1. The patient must be a woman with osteoporosis who meets the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit.
2. The patient must have a bone fracture that a doctor certifies was related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
3. The patient's doctor must certify that they cannot give themselves the injection or learn how to give themselves the drug by injection.
4. Medicare will cover the home health nurse or aide to provide the injection if the patient's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to give them the drug by injection.

In this case, Medicare will cover injectable osteoporosis drugs if the patient meets all of the above conditions.``
