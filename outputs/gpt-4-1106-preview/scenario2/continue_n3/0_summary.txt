1. IVIG must be provided in the patient's home to be covered.
2. The patient must have a diagnosis of primary immune deficiency disease for coverage eligibility.
3. The patient's doctor must determine that receiving IVIG at home is medically appropriate.
4. Medicare Part B covers the cost of IVIG.
5. Medicare Part B does not cover the cost of other items and services related to the administration of IVIG at home.
