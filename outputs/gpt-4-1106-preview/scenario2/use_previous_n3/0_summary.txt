1. IVIG Coverage: Medicare will cover Intravenous Immune Globulin (IVIG) when administered at home under the following conditions:
   a. The patient must have a diagnosis of primary immune deficiency disease.
   b. The patient's doctor must determine that it is medically appropriate for the IVIG to be administered at home.
2. Part B Coverage: Medicare Part B specifically covers the cost of the IVIG medication.
3. Exclusions: Medicare Part B does not cover additional items or services related to the administration of IVIG at home.
