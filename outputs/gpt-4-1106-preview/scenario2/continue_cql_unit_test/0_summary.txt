1. IVIG Coverage: Medicare will cover Intravenous Immune Globulin (IVIG) when administered at home under the following conditions:
   a. The patient must have a diagnosis of primary immune deficiency disease.
   b. The patient's doctor must determine that it is medically appropriate for the patient to receive IVIG at home.
2. Part B Inclusions: Medicare Part B covers the cost of the IVIG medication itself.
3. Part B Exclusions: Medicare Part B does not cover the cost of other items and services related to the administration of IVIG at home.
