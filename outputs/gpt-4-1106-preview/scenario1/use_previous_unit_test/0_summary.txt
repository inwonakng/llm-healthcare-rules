1. Injectable osteoporosis drugs are covered by Medicare under certain conditions.
2. The insured must be a woman diagnosed with osteoporosis.
3. The insured must meet the criteria for the Medicare home health benefit.
4. The insured must have a bone fracture certified by a doctor as related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
5. The insured's doctor must certify that the insured is unable to self-administer the injection or learn to do so.
6. Medicare will also cover a home health nurse or aide to provide the injection if:
   6.1. The insured's family or caregivers are unable to administer the drug by injection.
   6.2. The insured's family or caregivers are unwilling to administer the drug by injection.
