1. Injectable osteoporosis drugs are covered if the following conditions are met:
   1.1. The patient is a woman with osteoporosis who qualifies for the Medicare home health benefit.
   1.2. The patient has a bone fracture that a doctor has certified as related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
   1.3. The patient's doctor has certified that the patient is unable to self-administer the injection or learn to self-inject.
2. Medicare will also cover a home health nurse or aide to administer the injection if:
   2.1. The patient's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the drug by injection.
