1. Injectable osteoporosis drugs are covered by Medicare if the following conditions are met:
   1.1. The patient is a woman diagnosed with osteoporosis.
   1.2. The patient qualifies for the Medicare home health benefit.
   1.3. The patient has experienced a bone fracture certified by a doctor as related to post-menopausal osteoporosis.
   1.4. The patient's doctor certifies that the patient is unable to self-administer the injection or learn to do so.
2. Medicare will also cover the services of a home health nurse or aide to administer the injection if:
   2.1. The patient's family or caregivers are unable or unwilling to administer the drug by injection.
   2.2. This coverage is contingent upon the patient meeting all the conditions listed in requirement 1.
