"Summary:

Medicare covers injectable osteoporosis drugs for women with osteoporosis who meet specific conditions. The requirements are:
1. Must be a woman with osteoporosis who qualifies for the Medicare home health benefit
2. Must have a bone fracture certified by a doctor as being related to post-menopausal osteoporosis
3. Must have certification from the doctor stating inability to self-administer the injection or learn how to do so
4. Medicare will cover a home health nurse or aide to administer the injection if family or caregivers are unable or unwilling."
