Summary:
Medicare covers Intravenous Immune Globulin (IVIG) if certain conditions are met. The coverage is for individuals with a primary immune deficiency disease who require IVIG treatment at home.

Requirements:
1. You must have a diagnosis of primary immune deficiency disease.
2. Your doctor must determine that it is medically appropriate for you to receive IVIG treatment at home.
3. Medicare Part B covers the cost of IVIG itself.
4. Other items and services related to the IVIG treatment at home are not covered by Medicare.
